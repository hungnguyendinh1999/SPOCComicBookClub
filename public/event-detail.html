Science-Fiction People of Clark

SPOC is a student-run organization from Clark University that serves to feed all interests affiliated with "nerd" culture. In accomplishing this, we host numerous events, including weekly game nights, anime showcase nights, Magic the Gathering events, creative campus events, a field trip to King Richard's (Rennaisance) Faire, and even an in-house convention!.
